Safety Toolkit

Prepare. Protect. Persist.

Essential safety resources organized by when you need them. Download guides for offline access—because connectivity isn't always guaranteed.

1

Before Action

  • Write emergency contact info on your arm in permanent marker
  • Charge your phone fully; bring a portable battery
  • Set up encrypted messaging (Signal) with your affinity group
  • Know the number of a local legal aid hotline
  • Designate a trusted contact who is NOT attending
  • Review the legal observer guidelines for your area
2

During Action

  • Stay with your buddy or affinity group at all times
  • Document everything but protect identities of participants
  • Know your exits and have a meeting point if separated
  • Stay hydrated and carry basic first aid supplies
  • Use airplane mode when not actively communicating
  • If detained: state clearly "I am exercising my right to remain silent"
3

After Action

  • Check in with your group—physical and emotional wellness
  • Scrub metadata from photos before sharing online
  • Document any rights violations while memory is fresh
  • Rest. Recovery is not optional—it's strategic
  • Connect with support resources if feeling overwhelmed
  • Debrief with your team: what worked, what to improve
